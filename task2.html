Mohamed Salah

The Story Of A Hero

Mohamed Salah (born June 15, 1992, Basyoun, Egypt) Egyptian soccer (association football) player known for his prolific scoring,which made him an invaluable contributor to both the English Premier League and the Egyptian national team. Salah was named 2017 African Footballer of the Year and the Premier League’s player of the season in 2017–18 after scoring 32 goals in a single season as a forward for Liverpool FC. Salah is a three-time recipient of the Golden Boot, the award for the Premier League’s top scorer of the season.
